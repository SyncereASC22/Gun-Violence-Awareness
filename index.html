OUR MISSION

We want reform.

As a team, our mission is to build awareness over gun issues in three different ways. Firstly, we want to give people the access to sites where donations can be made to Gun Violence Awareness charities and advocates who can help provide change on a larger scale. Secondly, we want to provide people with centralized stats on not only gun violence specifically in their area, but all over the US as well. And lastly, we want to educate people on faulty gun laws so they can get the idea of changing gun laws to their state representative.
